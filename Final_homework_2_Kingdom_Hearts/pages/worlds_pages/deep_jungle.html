Deep Jungle

Deep Jungle is a world that appears only in Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts Final Mix, based on the 1999 Disney film, Tarzan.

It is home to Tarzan and his ape tribe, as well as a group of explorers trying to learn more about the gorillas.
