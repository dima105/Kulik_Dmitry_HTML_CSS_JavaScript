Hollow bastion

It is not based on a Disney film, though it shows Disney influence as it is similar to the Beast's haunted castle in Beauty & the Beast, and Maleficent's fortress from the Forbidden Mountain in Sleeping Beauty. This is supported by the presence of characters from both movies.

Hollow Bastion is a colossal castle that towers above the rest of its world, Radiant Garden, once being quite beautiful in appearance, but since then had one side extremely modified that had caused it to look twisted. It remained as such until repairs began prior to Kingdom Hearts II. At one point, it is the only remaining part of the city.
